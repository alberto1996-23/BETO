Mission

With my education, I will work within the software engineering career field to gain some real world experience. I have gained many soft skillsets in prior jobs, and I have the confidence and attitude which will carry me to success in my life. I also want to use what I learned in school to reach out to kids and teens in impoverished neighborhoods who don't have access to any STEM-related education in schools or centers due to city neglect and underfunding. I also attended a school which was underfunded and couldn't provide students with access to college and career-readying curriculars such as AP computer science education. Though self-determination and motivation, I was able to learn about the field utlizing free resources, which gained me access to computer science major at my college. I hope to pass that education and passion down to give those with less accessibility a chance.
